Our Admissions Process

1

Inquiry & Tour

Begin by submitting an inquiry form or scheduling a campus tour. Virtual tours available for international families.

2

Submit Application

Complete our online application and upload required documents (transcripts, recommendations, etc.).

3

Student Assessment

All applicants complete an age-appropriate assessment (academic testing or classroom observation).

4

Family Interview

Meet with our admissions team to discuss your child's needs and the school's philosophy.

5

Admission Decision

Receive notification within 3 weeks of completing all steps. Accepted students receive enrollment packages.
