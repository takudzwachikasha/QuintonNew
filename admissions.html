Financial Aid & Scholarships

We believe financial circumstances should not prevent qualified students from accessing quality education.

Quinton Group of Schools offers several financial assistance options:

  • Merit Scholarships: For students with exceptional academic records
  • Need-Based Aid: For families demonstrating financial need
  • Special Talent Awards: For students excelling in arts, sports, or leadership
  • Alumni Scholarships: For children of Quinton alumni

Applications for financial aid are considered separately from admissions applications and require additional documentation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the student-to-teacher ratio at Quinton?

Our average class sizes are:

  • Early Years: 15 students with 1 teacher and 1 assistant
  • Primary School: 20 students per class
  • Secondary School: 22-25 students per class

These ratios allow for personalized attention while maintaining a dynamic classroom environment.

Do you accept mid-term transfers?

Yes, we consider mid-term transfers based on availability. The admissions process is the same, and we require all previous school records. Please contact our admissions office to discuss potential openings.

What are the school hours?

Our standard school hours are:

  • Early Years: 8:00 AM - 12:30 PM (with optional extended care until 3:00 PM)
  • Primary School: 7:30 AM - 1:00 PM
  • Secondary School: 7:30 AM - 2:30 PM

After-school activities and clubs typically run until 4:00 PM.

Is transportation provided?

Yes, we offer optional bus transportation services covering most areas in Chitungwiza and surrounding neighborhoods. Routes and fees vary depending on your location. Our transportation coordinator can provide specific details during the enrollment process.

How does Quinton support students with learning differences?

Our Learning Support Department provides individualized assistance for students with mild to moderate learning differences. Services include:

  • Individualized Education Plans (IEPs)
  • Small group instruction
  • Classroom accommodations
  • Collaboration with outside specialists

We recommend scheduling a meeting with our learning support coordinator to discuss your child's specific needs.
