About Quinton Group of Schools

A legacy of excellence in education since 1985, nurturing future leaders across our global campuses.

Our Mission

To provide a transformative education that empowers students to excel academically, think critically, and contribute meaningfully to a rapidly evolving world.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity and ethical leadership
  • Innovation in learning
  • Global citizenship
  • Inclusive community

Our History

1985

Foundation of Quinton Academy

Established in London as a single-campus institution focusing on STEM education.

2002

First International Campus

Expanded to Dubai with a flagship campus offering IB and British curricula.

2015

Global Recognition

Ranked among the top 100 international schools by Education World Magazine.

2023

Present Day

12 campuses worldwide, 10,000+ students, and a network of 30,000+ alumni.
