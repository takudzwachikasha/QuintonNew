About Quinton Group of Schools

Giving Your Child a Headstart to Greatness

Our Story

Founded in 1998, Quinton Group of Schools has been a beacon of educational excellence in Chitungwiza for over two decades.

What began as a small neighborhood school has grown into a comprehensive educational institution serving students from early childhood through secondary education. Our commitment to academic rigor, character development, and community engagement has made us one of the most respected schools in the region.

Our motto, "Giving Your Child a Headstart to Greatness," reflects our belief that every child deserves access to quality education that prepares them for success in an increasingly complex world.

Our Journey

1998 - Humble Beginnings

Quinton Group of Schools was founded with just 35 students in a small building in Chitungwiza, offering primary education to the local community.

2005 - First Expansion

We opened our secondary school division in response to growing demand from parents who wanted their children to continue with our educational approach.

2012 - New Campus

We moved to our current location at 18361 Rockview West, featuring modern classrooms, science labs, and sports facilities.

2018 - 20th Anniversary

Celebrated two decades of educational excellence with over 1,200 alumni making impacts in various fields across Zimbabwe and beyond.

2023 - Present Day

Now serving over 800 students annually with a reputation for academic excellence and holistic development.
